Our Story

A Different Kind of Wild

Nestled in the lush jungles of northern Costa Rica, Rio Celeste Jungle Glamping was born from one simple belief: that luxury and nature aren't opposites — they're partners.

Wake up to toucans calling, fall asleep to the jungle orchestra. Spend your days riding horses through the canopy, tubing the legendary blue river, and hiking trails that few tourists ever see. Come home to your geodesic dome — spacious, stylish, and impossibly comfortable.

Adventures Await

Horseback Riding

Ride through the jungle canopy on guided horseback tours that wind down to the legendary Rio Celeste river.

🚣

River Tubing

Float down the turquoise waters of Rio Celeste on an exhilarating tubing adventure unlike anything else on earth.

🥾

Jungle Hiking

Trek through ancient jungle trails with local guides, discovering hidden waterfalls, exotic plants, and breathtaking river views.

🐦

Wildlife Watching

Spot toucans, scarlet macaws, howler monkeys, and over 100 bird species right from your dome or on guided wildlife walks.

Swim in the iconic turquoise waters of Rio Celeste — one of the world's most uniquely colored rivers, just minutes away.
