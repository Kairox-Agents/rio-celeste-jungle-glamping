Our Story

A Different Kind
of Wild

Nestled in the lush jungles of northern Costa Rica, Rio Celeste Jungle Glamping was born from a single conviction: that real luxury and raw nature aren't opposites — they're the same thing.

Wake to the call of howler monkeys and toucans. Spend your days tubing the legendary turquoise river, riding horses through ancient jungle, and hiking trails tourists rarely find. Return to your geodesic dome — spacious, beautiful, impossible to forget.
